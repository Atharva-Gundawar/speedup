Privacy Policy for Video Speed Controller Extension

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

Video Speed Controller does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

2. How We Use Information

The extension only modifies the playback speed of HTML5 videos on your local browser. No data is sent to any external servers.

3. Permissions Used

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access and modify video elements on the current webpage
  • scripting: To inject the speed control interface into webpages

4. Data Storage

The extension may store your preferred video speed settings locally in your browser's storage. This data is not transmitted anywhere and remains on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Video Speed Controller does not use any third-party services or analytics.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/Atharva-Gundawar/speedup
